Homemade Gelato

Cool, creamy, and smooth — this homemade gelato is a taste of Italy in every spoonful. Made with a simple custard base, it’s the perfect canvas for any flavor you can dream up, from classic vanilla to rich chocolate or fresh seasonal fruits. Once you try it, you’ll never want store-bought ice cream again.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 egg yolks
  • ½ cup sugar

Steps

  1. Mix milk and cream in a medium saucepan. Warm until foam forms around the edges. Remove from heat.
  2. Beat egg yolks and sugar until frothy. Gradually pour warm milk into egg yolks and sugar, whisking constantly. Return mixture to saucepan; cook until thickened and coats the back of the spoon.
  3. Pour through a sieve into a bowl. Cover and chill 2 hours to overnight.
  4. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze per instructions. Transfer to sealed container and freeze until firm.
